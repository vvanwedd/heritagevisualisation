Welcome to pixel+'s documentation!!
==================================

These technical documents are the result of the pixel+ project, which aims to bring RTI and PLD, both single camera multi light scanning techniques closer together. This document describes both technologies and focusses especially on their output data and provides background information, aiding in the interpretation of the output data.
